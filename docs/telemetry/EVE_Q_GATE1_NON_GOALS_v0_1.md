# EVE_Q++ Gate 1 Non-Goals v0.1

This pilot does not implement:

- live proposal generation;
- route selection from live data;
- wallet discovery or access;
- signing;
- order construction or submission;
- transaction construction or broadcast;
- capital movement;
- autonomous promotion;
- testnet execution;
- live-capital execution;
- Gate 2 or later activation.

The pilot is limited to read-only observation, content-addressed storage, freshness checks, offline replay, and rollback evidence.
